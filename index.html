About

I am a PhD student in statistics and machine learning jointly at Université de Montpellier, INRAE Montpellier and OVGU Magdeburg, working with my supervisors Joseph Salmon, Nicolas Verzelen and Alexandra Carpentier.

My thesis consists in finding the theoretical optimal detection bounds for different statistical models such as change-point detection or crowd labelling.
